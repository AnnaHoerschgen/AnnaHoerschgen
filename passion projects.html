Passion Projects

Before we begin, I find it important to find this proves how I work on my own rather than how I conduct myself doing it. I find it necessary that there is a much-needed disconnect from art and artist. You can like something and NOT like it's creator (or vice versa), that is one of my personal philosophies. So, don't take these as a judgement of my character or work ethic, as some of the presented pieces are relatively ancient and/or dated when compared to my modern works.
